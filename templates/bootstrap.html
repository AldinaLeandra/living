The Fair Wage

How well do you pay your household workers?

Assessing what comprises a fair compensation for your domestic employee can be tricky. Often times we pay our domestic employees what 'feels right', discuss rates with neighbours, or even just pay minimum wage - which is roughly a miniscule R80 per day (as of 2014). This tool is meant to help you make an informed decision on what a fair rate of pay is given the expenses South Africans face (2014).




You effectively pay R1880 per month

* We assume full time employment at your rate of pay. This implies six days a week and a little over four weeks a month.
